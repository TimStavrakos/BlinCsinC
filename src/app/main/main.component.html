Welcome to BlinCsinC!

This application lets you evoke emotions through displaying images and capturing them on camera.

There is a library where you can view all the images and emotions used to evoke a reaction.

There is also a Take Picture tab where it will take you step by step on how to display an image and take the picture!

Once you take some photos, you can upload your photos to your computer by hitting the button below.

Doing so will log you out of the application.
